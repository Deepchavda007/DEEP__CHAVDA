YOLOv8 AUTO TRAIN

YOLO_AUTO_TRAIN is a Python script that revolutionizes the training process for the advanced YOLOv8 object detection model. With its innovative features, it simplifies and automates key aspects of training, saving valuable time and effort.

First, it streamlines the creation of YAML files, which are essential for configuring the training parameters and dataset specifications. Wav2Lip, Codeformer GANs

Wav2Lip CodeFormer

Wav2Lip, Codeformer GANs

Wav2Lip CodeFormer

The goal of this project is to combine the power of two state-of-the-art deep learning techniques, namely Wav2Lip and Codeformer GANs, to enhance videos by achieving accurate lip synchronization and improving video quality. By leveraging the capabilities of both models, we aim to generate realistic lip movements that are in sync with the audio while enhancing the overall video appearance.

Combining Wav2Lip and Codeformer GANs, we unlock a realm of possibilities for transforming videos into captivating and immersive experiences. Wav2Lip excels in generating highly realistic lip movements by mapping audio to video frames, ensuring an unparalleled level of synchronization between speech and visual cues. On the other hand, Codeformer GANs significantly enhance video quality through advanced generative adversarial network techniques.
